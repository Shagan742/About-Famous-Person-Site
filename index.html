Queen Mary I

Queen of England
1516-1558




Early Life

  • Parents: King Henry VIII and Catherine of Aragon
  • Was a bright girl until her dad divorced mom due to feeling relationship has incest
  • Dad marries Anne Boleyn, who both birth Elizabeth I and consider Mary I as lady-in-waiting.
  • Mary I witnesses dad divorcing a bunch of wives, with her title changing. She turns into Prince Edward’s godmother after Henry married Jane Seymour, the third queen.
  • Eventually, Mary I is the queen of England.

Societal Contributions

image
  • Was a bold but more forgiving queen than King Henry VII.
  • Married King Phillip II of Spain to bring people back to the Church of Rome.
  • Wanted to bring back Roman Catholicism, but later had enemies, the Protestants.
  • Lived a very sad life because she had no kids, was hated by everyone, and nicknamed “Bloody Mary”

Legacy

  • Known for reversing Protestant Reformation back into Catholicism.
  • James I was King of Scots to King of England related to Mary I. From him, every British monarch is descended from Mary I.
